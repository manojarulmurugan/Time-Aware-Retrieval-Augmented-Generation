Research Demo

Standard dense retrieval is temporally blind. Contriever finds semantically similar passages regardless of era, so queries about 1850 can surface passages from 1920. This system fine-tunes Contriever with temporal triplet training and applies MRAG re-ranking to anchor retrieval to the correct historical period.
